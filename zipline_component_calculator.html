Public basis for defaults: Zipline says it safely makes a delivery every 30 seconds somewhere in the world, which is about 2,880 deliveries per day. A Ghana rollout described 600 deliveries per day using 30 drones, implying about 20 deliveries per drone per day in that operating setup. Those numbers support an operations proxy, not a confirmed manufacturing rate.
